The cafeteria presents many opportunities for nutrition education and promotion. Regardless of space or size, the cafeteria is a hub for school meals, socialization, and education! How can the cafeteria be used as a learning space? Children learn to try new foods through exposure. Taste tests, Harvest of the Month and highlighting new menu options are all great ways to introduce new foods to students.
