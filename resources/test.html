Students can be tested and assessed online

By Group5| July 28, 2021 | Article

If because of conditions, it is not possible to go to school directly, as is happening in many localities recently, students can be tested and assessed online."

That is the assertion of Deputy Minister of Education and Training Nguyen Huu Do, when clarifying more about the content of the document No. 5766/BGDĐT-GDTT issued on December 13, 2021, to guide the organization of teaching, evaluating primary school students' implementation of the general education program in line with the progress of the Covid-19 epidemic.

In localities where students cannot go to school due to the epidemic, schools organize regular and periodic assessment for students in the online form. For areas that have fully met the epidemic prevention requirements, students can go to school to study, schools urgently review and consolidate knowledge for students and conduct regular assessment. , periodical directly.

For students in grades 1 and 2 who are learning through television, their learning process lacks interaction, and the school and teachers have not yet accurately assessed the children's acquisition of knowledge. wake up anywhere. Therefore, in the allowable conditions, it is necessary to create the maximum conditions for students to go to school to consolidate their knowledge and be guided to take direct periodic tests. For grades 3, 4, and 5, students basically have a sense of and study skills, and skills to take periodic tests according to the characteristics of each subject in the learning process from previous years.

The point of view of the Ministry of Education and Training is that depending on the actual conditions in the localities, the departments and divisions of education direct the schools to guide the periodic examination and assessment by face-to-face or online form. At the same time, localities can flexibly adjust the time of testing in accordance with this school year's development plan, the new school year plan and the epidemic situation in their localities.
